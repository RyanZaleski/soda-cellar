Soda Cellar are one of the UK's leading suppliers and distributors of post mix syrups.

Our goal is to provide our customers with premium quality post mix drinks and soft drinks dispensing equipment of the highest quality, all whilst maintaining the best possible customer service.

We have been providing high quality post mix products and equipment to pubs, bars, clubs, schools, shops and other hospitality sectors since establishing in 2017.

We are based in Maldon, Essex, but have customers all over Dagenham, Cambridge, Hertfordshire and London. To find out if we delivery to your area, please contact our sales team.

We are also a sister company to Essex Industrial Gases LTD, who supply Argon, Helium and Propane to over 2000 customers across Essex, Suffolk, London and surrounding areas.

We are committed to providing the best possible service for our customers.
