Soda Cellar offer a broad range of high quality post mix products.

We offer big-brand beverages like Pepsi, 7up and Tango as well as our own range of syrups, cordials and mixers. We also offer products that are low in sugar and also no-sugar products

Most post mix solutions are generally set at a 5 to 1 ratio, meaning 5 litres of soda water for every 1 litre of post mix syrup. However there are some types that use different strengths.

Our products are manufactured carefully to combine the best raw ingrediants resulting in high qualty post mix at great low prices.

We are always expanding our products range, so if you would like to know if we stock a product that you are looking for, contact our sales team.
